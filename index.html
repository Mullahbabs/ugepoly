About Us

Drive to Educational Excellence

Established with a vision to promote technical education and skill development, Federal Polytechnic Ugep stands as a beacon of academic excellence in Nigeria. Our institution is committed to equipping students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

At Federal Polytechnic Ugep, we offer a wide range of programs across various fields, including engineering, business, technology, and the arts. Our curriculum is designed to blend theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of their chosen careers.

Mission

To provide quality technical and vocational education that empowers students with practical skills, innovative knowledge, and ethical values setting the stage for internation standards.

Vision

To be a leading polytechnic institution in Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, innovative research, and industry-driven programs that produce competent, skilled, and globally competitive graduates.

Integrity

Integrity is the cornerstone of our operations. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our academic and administrative processes.

Values

We hold ourselves responsible for our actions and decisions, ensuring that we maintain the trust and confidence of students, staff, and stakeholders.
